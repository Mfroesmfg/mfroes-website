About Us

Built by People Who Know Seed

Founded in 1999, MFroes is a family-owned business with over 25 years of experience in the seed and grain industry. Built on hard work and integrity, we've played a key role in enhancing product cleanliness, gaining invaluable insight into efficient, excellent seed processing along the way.

MFroes was started in 1999 by Cornelius Froes and his father, Peter. For much of his life, Peter owned and ran a bean and wheat cleaning facility, and that's where Cornelius, as a kid, first started working and helping out at his side. Peter taught him the trade hands-on, passing down everything he knew about cleaning seed. Together, father and son turned that lifetime of knowledge into MFroes.

From our shop in Cuauhtémoc, Chihuahua, we combine proven craftsmanship with North American engineering to supply, fabricate, and refurbish seed processing machinery. We're committed to quality, integrity, and innovation, because your success is our priority.

How It Works

From Raw Harvest to Clean Seed

A clean line is a series of simple, deliberate steps. Here's how our equipment turns raw harvest into product you're proud to put your name on.

Raw harvest in Clean seed out
Step 01

Clean & Aspirate

Screens and airflow pull chaff, straw, dust, and light trash off the top.

Step 02

Size & Grade

Multiple decks grade by width and thickness for a uniform sample.

Step 03

Separate & De-Stone

Density separation floats out stones, mud balls, and light seed.

Step 04

Convey & Handle

Elevators and conveyors move product gently, ready to bag or store.
